Tutorial List

Below are a list of the training videos that are available. They call into three categories to address these three audiences:

  • Circle Members: people who are members of a circle, participating by attending meetings, adding and accessing various resources for the circle.
  • Circle Leaders: people who are leading a circle, who are concerned with managing who has access to the circle the resources.
  • Site Owners: people who manage the site, and provide all the workspaces for the various circles.

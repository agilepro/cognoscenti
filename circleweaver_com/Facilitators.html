Circle Weaver Facilitators

The following Circle Weaver facilitators are ready to support your organization's growth and success. Each has been certified and can help your organization reorganize and operate more efficiently. They are also ready to help you set up your site, your workspaces, and weave your group together through efficient tracking, clear communication, and the ability to share resources.
They are ready to support your organization's growth and more.

John Buck

John brought sociocracy to the English-speaking world beginning in 1990. He continues to enjoy encouraging the development of organic organizations that operate with a lively, multi-hearted sense of being. He resides in Silver Spring, Maryland, USA with his wife, Ramona, an internationally certified mediator.

Angelika Pohnitzer

Angelika is Leadership Associates’ Executive Director. She is a licensed leadership and management advisor, certified auditor, facilitator, trainer and coach. In her work, she focuses on inclusive, positive and agile leadership based on personal and organizational integrity with a view to sustainability. She is passionate about social innovation, entre/intrapreneurship, governance, management systems and digital tools. Angelika has an academic background in Sociology, Criminology, International Relations and Integrated Risk Management from Austria and the UK. Her experience ranges from civil society – including faith-based organizations – to small businesses, from large international companies to international non-profits. Angelika is based in Vienna from where she supports Leadership Associates’ engagement in Austria, Germany and Switzerland. She is equally happy to work online, which she does frequently, e.g. in her positions on the Strategy Boards of Governance Alive and the Institute for Integral Studies in Freiburg, Germany.

angelika.pohnitzer@leadership.associates

Vienna, Austria

https://leadership.associates/

Swarnalakshmi Ravi

Swarnalakshmi R, is a motivational speaker and holds two master degrees, one in Political Science from the University of Madras in India and another in international relations and global governance from Aston University in England, where she received one of ten scholarships for academic excellence from over 1000 applicants and is the first person from India ever to receive one of the International Vice Chancellor scholarships. She became A certified facilitator in sociocracy Governance Method at the age of 22 after receiving training from Governance Alive. Swarnalakshmi held the position of National Child Prime Minister and other positions of responsibility in the Inclusive Neighbourhood Children’s Parliaments movement of India, which also uses sociocracy as its governance method. Experienced in networking and team building through working with the children’s parliaments for 13 years, she helped to build a board software for children to efficiently organize and conduct meetings. At the age of 21, she participated in making 2 app pitches to the Venture Capitalists of Silicon Valley and EBAN. She has addressed the United Nations four times as a keynote speaker at the ages of 13, 14, 16, and 17, advocating for Inclusive Education and the prevention of violence against women and girls, and continues to lobby with the ambassadors and diplomats of about 20 countries. At present, she supports the World Children’s Parliament as a Global Convenor, networking with children from across different continents whenever possible..

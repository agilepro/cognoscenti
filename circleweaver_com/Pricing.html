Step One: Sign up for a Circle Weaver Site

CircleWeaver is a web-based application that allows you to create a site for your organization.

You can create and manage Workspaces for each committee or working group.

Your CircleWeaver Site will grow with your organization...

And you can start for free!

Your Circle Weaver Site comes with everything you need:

1 CircleWeaver Workspace

The Workspace supports a group of people working together on a project.

1 Workspace Administrator

The Administrator manages and makes organizational changes to the site and can serve as a steward in any Workspace within the site.

Plus up to 20 Members!

Unpaid users with read-only access will still be able to attend meetings, access materials, receive email, and participate in the group with comments and responses to questions.

This is all you need
to run a small organization

Grow your Circle Weaver Site with additional Workspaces!

Need an Additional Active Workspace?          $2 per month

With each paid (Active) Workspace, you get 4 additional Frozen Workspaces.
Only Active Workspaces are charged. Workspaces can be frozen at any time and then cost nothing.

Need Additional Stewards?          $1 per month

Additional paid users can be added to any Workspace at any time.
Administrators can set limits on the number of paid users allowed in the site.

Pay for only what you need, and let CircleWeaver help grow your organization!
